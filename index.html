AI Meal Planning

Your AI chef looks at what's in your fridge, your cupboard, and your shopping list — then builds you a full meal plan on the spot. No guessing, no waste, no "what's for dinner?" ever again.

💡 Pro tip: Keep your lists up to date — add items as you stock up and check them off as you run out. The more your AI knows about your kitchen, the smarter your meal plans get.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

Three simple steps to a smarter shopping experience.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up for free in seconds. Set your display name and you're ready to go.

2

Build Your Lists

Create monthly or utility lists, add categories, and start adding items. Invite family to share in real time.

3

Shop, Cook & Let AI Do the Rest

Check off items as you shop — online or offline. Tell your AI what's in your fridge, cupboard, and list, and it generates a full meal plan instantly. Add items as you stock up, check them off as you run out, and your AI gets smarter with every shop.
